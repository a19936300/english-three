## Part A 应答模板

## 1. Hometown（家乡）

(T=teacher S=student)

T: Do you live near here?

S: Yes, I do./No, I live in +居住地.

T: Where do you live?

S: I live in this city./I live in +居住地.

T: What do you think are the good points about living there?

S: The weather is comfortable and the people are kind. Most importantly, many of my friends are here.

T: How do you usually go to work/school? Why?

S: I often go to work/school by bus/train. Because it's very cheap and convenient for me.

T: Where did you live before you came here?

S: I lived in +居住地.

T: How long have you lived here?

S: I have been living here for x years.

T: How do you like it? Why?

S: I like it very much. Because the city is clean and the air is fresh.

## 2. Leisure（业余时间）

(T=teacher S=student)

T: Do you have any hobbies?

S: Yes. I like reading/playing basketball/playing computer games.

T: What do you usually do in your spare time?

S: I often go shopping with my friends.

或 I often do some sports.

或 I often watch some movies from my cell phone.

或 I often go to travel in other cities.

## 3. Family（家庭）

(T=teacher S=student)

T: Can you tell us something about your family?

S: Of course. There are x people in my family: my father, my mother and I. My father is a doctor. My mother is a teacher. And I am a +职业.

T: What does your family usually do for the weekend?

S: We often stay together at home watching TV. Sometimes we go out for eating.

T: Do you enjoy it? Why?

S: Yes, I like my family very much. We care and look after each other in daily life. My parents and I always understand each other. I feel lucky being their child.

T: What do you think about living together with parents/by yourself?

S: Every coin has two sides. I prefer to living with my parents. For the one hand, we can stay more time being together. For the other hand, it's time for me to look after them.

## 4. Work（工作）

(T=teacher S=student)

T: Can you tell me something about your job?

S: I am a +职业. I have been worked for x years. I like my job. Because my colleagues are nice. Although the job is challenging, I can learn many things and improve myself.

T: What do you enjoy most about your job?

S: (I am a +职业.) The job has many good points. But what makes me enjoy most is it can improve my abilities.

T: What do you dislike about your work?

S: It's very far away from my place. And I can't afford the expensive rent near my company. So every day, I have to spend three hours to commute.

T: Do you have any future plans?

S: Yes, I want to be promoted within two years. So I have been tried to improve myself by keeping studying.

## 5. Study（学习）

(T=teacher S=student)

T: Can you tell me something about your study?

S: I major in English. I have to attend many subjects like Math, Computer, and other English related ones. I can do well in English. But I just can’t find a way in math.

T: What do you enjoy most about your school?

S: The campus is big and clean. The teachers and schoolmates are very friendly. But what I like most is the studying atmosphere. It makes me study more and play less.

T: What do you dislike about your school?

S: The campus is big and clean. The teachers and schoolmates are very friendly. But what I dislike is there are too many internet bars, which makes many students lose interests in studying.

T: Do you have any future plans?

S: I want to be a +职业. So I have been tried to practice my abilities to being a good +职业.

## Part B 通用语句

## 1. 常见图片种类及对应问题

有故事情节，连贯性的四幅图进行讨论（很少）

• What does the first picture show?

• What does the second picture show?

• What does the third picture show?

• What does the fourth picture show?

• What can you get from the four pictures?

对四个不同类型的活动、爱好、事物进行讨论（常用）

\- Which activity/one/图片上具体的事物 do you like best? And why?

\- Which activity/one/图片上具体的事物 do you dislike? And why?

\- Don't you like+某个具体的活动? And why?

• What do you think of the activity in picture x?

2. 没听清对方的表述，要求重述：I'm sorry. Can you please speak it slowly?

3. 对对方的观点进行回应:

You're right. But…（有不同观点的时候）
You're right. And…（补充对方观点的时候）

4. 引出自己观点的时候:
I think…Because…

5. 如果对方不停的说，你可以说：Excuse me! I'd like to say something about...

6. 结束会话的时候:
Different people have different opinions.

## Part C 通用语句

## 1. 主陈述者常用语句:

From the picture, we can see that+图片内容. It shows the phenomenon that +社会现象. (As the development of the society, 图片中反映的社会现象发生了怎样的变化.)

对于批判性很明显的社会现象

In my opinion, the reason(s) why some people +行为 is(are) that +原因. What we should do is +措施.

对于模棱两可的社会现象

Although there is a big discussion about it. Some people hold that +一种观点. Other people argue that+另一种观点.

However, as far as I am concerned, the phenomenon weight more advantages/disadvantages than disadvantages/advantages. Because+原因.

## 2. 次陈述者常用语句:

对于批判性很明显的社会现象

I am quiet agreed with you. But in addition what you have mentioned, I think we also should+1-2 条措施。

## 对于模棱两可的社会现象

What you have stated does make some sense. But in my opinion, 陈述自己的观点和理由. I can't agree with you any more. But I also think+补充自己的观点.
