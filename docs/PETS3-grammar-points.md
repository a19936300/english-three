## 1. 词法基础

### 1.1 名词

#### 1.1.1 可数名词复数规则

| 规则 | 变化方式 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 一般情况 | 加 -s | book → books, day → days |
| 以 s/x/ch/sh 结尾 | 加 -es | bus → buses, box → boxes |
| 辅音字母 + y 结尾 | 变 y 为 i 加 -es | city → cities, baby → babies |
| 以 f/fe 结尾 | 变 f/fe 为 v 加 -es | knife → knives, leaf → leaves |
| 以 o 结尾 | 有生命加 -es，无生命加 -s | potato → potatoes, photo → photos |

**不规则复数（高频考点）：**
- man → men, woman → women, child → children, foot → feet, tooth → teeth, goose → geese, mouse → mice, ox → oxen
- 单复数同形：sheep, deer, fish, means, species, series, Chinese, Japanese

#### 1.1.2 不可数名词

常考不可数名词：advice, information, news, furniture, equipment, luggage, homework, progress, weather, traffic, knowledge, evidence, damage

> ⚠️ **考点**：不可数名词不能加 -s，不能用 a/an 修饰，但可用 a piece of, an item of 等量词搭配。

#### 1.1.3 名词所有格

- **'s 所有格**：主要用于有生命的事物。Tom's book, the teacher's office
- **of 所有格**：主要用于无生命的事物。the door of the room
- **双重所有格**：a friend of my father's（我父亲的一个朋友）

> ⚠️ **考点**：时间、距离、国家、城市等虽无生命，也用 's 所有格：today's newspaper, five minutes' walk, Beijing's weather

---

### 1.2 冠词

#### 1.2.1 不定冠词 a/an

- **a** 用于辅音音素前：a university, a European country, a one-way street
- **an** 用于元音音素前：an hour, an honest boy, an umbrella

**基本用法：**
1. 泛指一类事物中的一个：I bought **a** book yesterday.
2. 表示"每一"：twice **a** week, 60 kilometers **an** hour
3. 固定搭配：a lot of, a few, a little, a number of, a variety of, as a result, in a hurry, have a good time

#### 1.2.2 定冠词 the

**基本用法：**
1. 特指：**The** book on the desk is mine.
2. 上文提到过的事物：I saw **a** film. **The** film was interesting.
3. 独一无二的事物：**the** sun, **the** earth, **the** moon
4. 序数词和最高级前：**the** first, **the** best
5. 乐器前：play **the** piano
6. 固定搭配：in the morning, by the way, on the other hand, in the end

#### 1.2.3 零冠词（不用冠词）

1. 专有名词前（一般情况）：China, Beijing, Tom
2. 不可数名词和复数名词表泛指：**Water** is essential. / **Dogs** are loyal.
3. 三餐、球类运动前：have **breakfast**, play **football**
4. 节日、月份、星期前：on **Monday**, in **January**, on **Christmas Day**
5. 称呼、头衔前：Doctor Wang, President Lincoln
6. 固定搭配：by bus, go to school, go to bed, at home, in fact, on foot

> ⚠️ **高频考点**：
> - **go to school**（上学）vs **go to the school**（去那所学校，不一定上学）
> - **in hospital**（住院）vs **in the hospital**（在医院里）
> - **at table**（在吃饭）vs **at the table**（在桌旁）

### 1.3 代词

#### 1.3.1 人称代词

| 人称 | 主格 | 宾格 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一人称单数 | I | me |
| 第二人称 | you | you |
| 第三人称单数 | he/she/it | him/her/it |
| 第一人称复数 | we | us |
| 第三人称复数 | they | them |

**并列人称代词排列顺序：**
- 单数：**you → he/she → I**（第二、三、一）
  - You, he and I should return on time.
- 复数：**we → you → they**（第一、二、三）
- 表示承担责任/道歉时，第一人称在前：I and Tom broke the window.

#### 1.3.2 物主代词

| 类型 | 形容词性 | 名词性 |
|------|----------|--------|
| 第一人称单数 | my | mine |
| 第二人称 | your | yours |
| 第三人称单数 | his/her/its | his/hers/its |
| 第一人称复数 | our | ours |
| 第三人称复数 | their | theirs |

> ⚠️ **考点**：形容词性物主代词后必须跟名词（my book）；名词性物主代词独立使用（It's mine）。

#### 1.3.3 反身代词

- 单数加 -self，复数加 -selves：myself, yourself, himself, herself, itself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves
- **常考搭配**：enjoy oneself, help oneself to, by oneself, teach oneself, devote oneself to, express oneself

#### 1.3.4 指示代词

- **this/these**（近指）vs **that/those**（远指）
- **that/those** 可代替前文提到的名词以避免重复：
  - The weather in Beijing is colder than **that** in Shanghai.（that = the weather）
  - The ears of a rabbit are longer than **those** of a cat.

#### 1.3.5 不定代词（重点）

| 不定代词 | 用法说明 |
|----------|----------|
| some / any | some 用于肯定句；any 用于否定句和疑问句（但期待肯定回答的疑问句用 some） |
| each / every | each 强调个体（两者或以上）；every 强调整体（三者或以上），后接单数名词 |
| both / all | both 两者都；all 三者及以上都 |
| neither / none | neither 两者都不；none 三者及以上都不 |
| either / any | either 两者中任一；any 三者及以上任一 |
| another / other / the other | another 另一个（三者以上）；the other 另一个（两者中的）；others 其他的（泛指） |
| few / a few | few 几乎没有（否定）；a few 有一些（肯定）——修饰可数名词 |
| little / a little | little 几乎没有（否定）；a little 有一些（肯定）——修饰不可数名词 |
| one | 泛指同类中的一个；替代前文出现的可数名词 |

> ⚠️ **高频考点**：
> - **one...the other**（两者中的一个...另一个）
> - **one...another**（三者以上中的一个...另一个）
> - **some...others**（一些...另一些，泛指）
> - **some...the others**（一些...其余的，特指）

### 1.4 形容词与副词

#### 1.4.1 形容词的位置

1. **前置定语**：多个形容词修饰同一名词时，常见顺序：
   > **限定词 → 观点 → 大小 → 形状 → 年龄 → 颜色 → 国籍 → 材料 → 用途 + 名词**
   - a beautiful small old round red Chinese wooden writing desk

2. **后置定语**：
   - 修饰 something, anything, everything, nothing 等不定代词时后置：something important
   - 表语形容词（alive, asleep, awake, alone, alike, aware）通常后置或作表语

#### 1.4.2 副词的分类与用法

| 类型 | 示例 | 位置 |
|------|------|------|
| 时间副词 | now, then, yesterday, already, yet, still | 句末或句中 |
| 频率副词 | always, usually, often, sometimes, seldom, never | 实义动词前，be/助动词后 |
| 程度副词 | very, quite, rather, fairly, extremely, too | 修饰的形容词/副词前 |
| 方式副词 | carefully, quickly, well, hard, fast | 通常放在动词后或句末 |

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - **hard**（副词，努力地）vs **hardly**（副词，几乎不）
> - **late**（迟地）vs **lately**（最近）
> - **near**（近）vs **nearly**（几乎）
> - **high**（高地）vs **highly**（高度地，非常）

#### 1.4.3 比较级和最高级

**规则变化：**

| 类型 | 构成方式 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 单音节 | 加 -er / -est | tall → taller → tallest |
| 以 e 结尾 | 加 -r / -st | large → larger → largest |
| 辅+元+辅 | 双写末尾字母加 -er/-est | big → bigger → biggest |
| 辅音+y | 变 y 为 i 加 -er/-est | happy → happier → happiest |
| 多音节/部分双音节 | 前加 more / most | beautiful → more beautiful → most beautiful |

**不规则变化：**
- good/well → better → best
- bad/badly/ill → worse → worst
- many/much → more → most
- little → less → least
- far → farther/further → farthest/furthest
- old → older/elder → oldest/eldest

**常考句型：**

| 句型 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| as + 原级 + as | 和...一样 | He is as tall as his father. |
| not as/so + 原级 + as | 不如... | He is not as/so tall as his father. |
| 比较级 + than | 比...更 | He is taller than his father. |
| the + 最高级 + in/of | 最... | He is the tallest in his class. |
| 比较级 + and + 比较级 | 越来越... | It's getting colder and colder. |
| the + 比较级, the + 比较级 | 越...就越... | The harder you work, the more you'll get. |
| one of the + 最高级 + 复数名词 | 最...之一 | He is one of the tallest students. |

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - 比较级前可用 much, far, a lot, a little, a bit, even, still, any 等修饰，但**不能用 very**。
> - 两者比较用比较级，三者及以上用最高级。
> - 比较对象要一致：The population of China is larger than **that** of India.（不能省略 that）

### 1.5 介词与介词短语

#### 1.5.1 时间介词

| 介词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| at | 时刻、时间点 | at 8 o'clock, at noon, at night, at the weekend |
| on | 具体某天、日期 | on Monday, on May 1st, on a rainy day |
| in | 较长时间段 | in 2024, in May, in the morning, in summer |
| for | 持续一段时间 | for two hours, for a long time |
| since | 从某个时间点起 | since 2020, since last Monday |
| by | 在...之前 | by the end of, by tomorrow |
| during | 在...期间 | during the summer vacation |

#### 1.5.2 地点介词

| 介词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| at | 小地点、具体位置 | at the bus stop, at home |
| in | 大地点、在...里面 | in Beijing, in the room |
| on | 在...表面上 | on the desk, on the wall |
| above/over | 在...上方 | The lamp is above/over the table. |
| below/under | 在...下方 | The cat is under the table. |
| between | 在两者之间 | between A and B |
| among | 在三者及以上之间 | among the students |

#### 1.5.3 常考介词短语搭配

**与动词搭配：**
- look at, look for, look after, look forward to
- listen to, belong to, refer to, stick to
- depend on, insist on, concentrate on
- consist of, take care of, get rid of
- apply for, account for, allow for
- come across, get across, put off, give up, turn down

**与形容词搭配：**
- be good at, be interested in, be proud of, be fond of
- be responsible for, be famous for, be different from
- be familiar with, be satisfied with, be similar to
- be afraid of, be tired of, be ashamed of

## 2. 动词核心

### 2.1 动词的分类

| 分类 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 实义动词 | 有实际意义，可单独作谓语 | run, eat, write |
| 系动词 | 连接主语和表语 | be, become, seem, look, feel, taste, smell, sound, remain, keep, turn, grow, get, go, appear |
| 助动词 | 帮助构成时态、语态、否定、疑问 | do, does, did, have, has, had, will, shall, be |
| 情态动词 | 表示能力、许可、推测等 | can, could, may, might, must, shall, should, will, would, need, dare, ought to |

> ⚠️ **系动词考点**：系动词后接形容词作表语，不接副词。
> - The food smells **good**.（✓）/ The food smells **well**.（✗，well 作副词时）

---

### 2.2 动词的时态

PETS-3 重点考查以下 8 种时态（16 种时态中最核心的部分）：

#### 2.2.1 一般现在时

**构成**：主语 + 动词原形 / 动词三单形式

**用法：**
1. 经常性、习惯性动作：I **get up** at 7 every morning.
2. 客观事实、真理：The earth **goes** around the sun.
3. 在时间/条件状语从句中代替将来时：If it **rains** tomorrow, we'll stay at home.
4. 时刻表、日程安排：The train **leaves** at 8:00 a.m.

#### 2.2.2 一般过去时

**构成**：主语 + 动词过去式

**用法：**
1. 过去某时的动作或状态：I **visited** Beijing last year.
2. 过去的习惯：She **went** to school by bus when she was young.

**常考时间标志词**：yesterday, last week/month/year, ago, in 2020, just now, the other day, used to

#### 2.2.3 一般将来时

**构成与用法：**

| 结构 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| will/shall + 动词原形 | 临时决定、预测、意愿 | I'll help you. It will rain tomorrow. |
| be going to + 动词原形 | 计划好的、有迹象表明的 | Look at the clouds. It's going to rain. |
| be about to + 动词原形 | 即将（不与时间状语连用） | The train is about to leave. |
| be to + 动词原形 | 按计划安排、命令 | The president is to visit Japan. |
| 现在进行时表将来 | 位移动词（come, go, leave, arrive, fly） | He is leaving tomorrow. |

#### 2.2.4 现在进行时

**构成**：am/is/are + doing

**用法：**
1. 现在正在进行的动作：I **am reading** a book now.
2. 近期一直在做的事：He **is writing** a novel these days.
3. 表示将来的安排（位移动词）：We **are leaving** tomorrow.
4. always/constantly + 进行时，表示不满/赞扬（感情色彩）：She **is always complaining**.

#### 2.2.5 过去进行时

**构成**：was/were + doing

**用法：**
1. 过去某时正在进行的动作：I **was watching** TV at 8 last night.
2. 与 when/while 连用：**While** I **was walking** in the park, I met an old friend.
3. 过去某个短暂动作发生时，另一个动作正在进行：The phone rang while I **was having** dinner.

#### 2.2.6 现在完成时

**构成**：have/has + 过去分词

**用法：**
1. 过去发生，对现在有影响：I **have lost** my key.（现在进不了门）
2. 从过去持续到现在：She **has lived** here since 2010.
3. 经历/经验：I **have been** to Beijing twice.

**常考时间标志词**：already, yet, just, ever, never, recently, lately, so far, up to now, since, for, in the past few years

> ⚠️ **高频考点**：
> - **have been to**（去过，已回来）vs **have gone to**（去了，还没回来）vs **have been in**（待在某地）
> - 现在完成时**不能**与 yesterday, last year, in 2020 等明确过去时间连用。
> - since + 过去时间点，for + 一段时间

#### 2.2.7 过去完成时

**构成**：had + 过去分词

**用法**（"过去的过去"）：
1. 过去某动作之前已完成的动作：When I arrived, the train **had left**.
2. by + 过去时间：By the end of last year, we **had learned** 2,000 words.
3. hope, think, expect, intend, mean, want 等的过去完成时，表示"未曾实现的愿望"：I **had hoped** to see you, but I was too busy.

> ⚠️ **考点**：before, after, by the time 引导的从句中，可用一般过去时代替过去完成时（因为时间先后已由连词表明）。

#### 2.2.8 现在完成进行时

**构成**：have/has been + doing

**用法**：从过去开始一直持续到现在的动作（强调持续性，可能还会继续）
- I **have been waiting** for you for two hours.
- She **has been studying** English since she was ten.

> ⚠️ **考点**：现在完成进行时 vs 现在完成时
> - I **have been reading** the book.（还没读完，强调过程）
> - I **have read** the book.（已读完，强调结果）

### 2.3 被动语态

#### 2.3.1 被动语态的构成

**基本结构**：be + 过去分词 (+ by + 动作执行者)

| 时态 | 被动语态结构 | 示例 |
|------|-------------|------|
| 一般现在时 | am/is/are + done | English **is spoken** worldwide. |
| 一般过去时 | was/were + done | The window **was broken** yesterday. |
| 一般将来时 | will be + done | The work **will be finished** soon. |
| 现在进行时 | am/is/are being + done | The road **is being repaired**. |
| 过去进行时 | was/were being + done | The car **was being washed** at that time. |
| 现在完成时 | have/has been + done | The letter **has been sent**. |
| 过去完成时 | had been + done | The house **had been sold** before we arrived. |
| 含情态动词 | 情态动词 + be + done | This **must be done** at once. |

#### 2.3.2 被动语态的用法

1. 不知道或没必要指出动作执行者：The bridge **was built** in 1990.
2. 强调动作的承受者：The song **was sung** by a famous singer.
3. 客观说明、科技文体：The experiment **was carried out** successfully.

#### 2.3.3 主动形式表被动（高频考点）

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 系动词 + 形容词 | The food tastes good. / The cloth feels soft. |
| want/need/require + doing | The car needs washing. = The car needs to be washed. |
| be worth doing | The book is worth reading. |
| 不定式作定语，与被修饰词构成动宾关系 | I have something to do. / Give me something to eat. |
| be + easy/difficult/hard/impossible + to do | The problem is easy to solve. |

### 2.4 虚拟语气

虚拟语气是 PETS-3 语法中的**难点和高频考点**。

#### 2.4.1 条件句中的虚拟语气

| 类型 | if 从句（条件） | 主句（结果） |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| 与现在事实相反 | did / were | would/could/might/should + do |
| 与过去事实相反 | had done | would/could/might/should + have done |
| 与将来事实相反 | did / were to do / should do | would/could/might/should + do |

**示例：**
- 与现在相反：If I **were** you, I **would accept** the offer.
- 与过去相反：If he **had studied** harder, he **would have passed** the exam.
- 与将来相反：If it **should rain** tomorrow, we **would cancel** the picnic.

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - **省略 if 的倒装**：Were I you... / Had he known... / Should it rain...
> - **错综时间条件句**：If I had taken your advice (过去), I would be better now (现在).

#### 2.4.2 名词性从句中的虚拟语气

**1. wish + 宾语从句**

| 时间 | 从句谓语 | 示例 |
|------|----------|------|
| 现在 | did / were | I wish I **were** taller. |
| 过去 | had done | I wish I **had studied** harder. |
| 将来 | would/could do | I wish it **would stop** raining. |

**2. suggest / demand / order / insist / recommend / propose / request / require 等动词后的宾语从句**
- 结构：(should) + 动词原形
- He suggested that we **(should) start** early.
- The doctor recommended that she **(should) take** more exercise.

> ⚠️ **注意**：insist 表"坚持说"时不用虚拟；suggest 表"表明"时不用虚拟。
> - He insisted that he **was** innocent.（坚持说，陈述语气）
> - He insisted that I **(should) go** there.（坚持要求，虚拟语气）

**3. It is + 形容词/过去分词 + that 从句 (should + 动词原形)**
- 常见词：important, necessary, essential, vital, urgent, strange, natural
- It is necessary that he **(should) attend** the meeting.
- It is suggested that the plan **(should) be carried out**.

**4. 其他虚拟结构**
- **would rather + 从句**：I would rather you **came** tomorrow.（现在/将来用过去时）/ I would rather you **hadn't done** that.（过去用过去完成时）
- **It's (high/about) time + 从句**：It's time you **went** to bed.（用过去时）
- **as if / as though**：He talks as if he **knew** everything.（与现在相反）/ He looked as if he **had seen** a ghost.（与过去相反）
- **if only**：If only I **were** rich! / If only I **had listened** to your advice!
- **but for**（要不是）：But for your help, I **would have failed**.

### 2.5 非谓语动词

非谓语动词是 PETS-3 **重点考查语法**之一，包括不定式、动名词和分词。

#### 2.5.1 不定式（to do）

**句法功能：**

| 功能 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 主语 | **To learn** a foreign language is important. / It is important **to learn** a foreign language.（it 作形式主语） |
| 宾语 | I want **to go** home. |
| 宾补 | I asked him **to help** me. / I saw him **cross** the road.（省略 to） |
| 定语 | I have a lot of work **to do**. / He is the first person **to arrive**. |
| 状语（目的） | He went to the station **to meet** his friend. |
| 状语（结果） | He woke up **to find** the house on fire.（意外结果） |

**省略 to 的不定式（感官动词/使役动词 + 宾语 + do）：**
- 感官动词：see, hear, watch, notice, feel, observe
- 使役动词：make, let, have
- 例：I saw him **cross** the road.（全过程）
- 但被动语态中要还原 to：He was seen **to cross** the road.

> ⚠️ **考点**：**to 是介词**的情况（后接动名词）：
> - look forward to **doing**, be used to **doing**, devote...to **doing**, pay attention to **doing**, object to **doing**, get down to **doing**, be accustomed to **doing**

#### 2.5.2 动名词（doing）

**句法功能：**

| 功能 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 主语 | **Swimming** is good exercise. |
| 宾语 | I enjoy **reading**. |
| 表语 | My hobby is **collecting** stamps. |
| 介词宾语 | I'm interested in **learning** English. |

**只接动名词的动词（高频考点）：**
- enjoy, finish, avoid, mind, suggest, consider, practice, keep, miss, delay, imagine, deny, admit, appreciate, risk, resist, give up, can't help, feel like, be worth, look forward to, be used to, object to

**只接不定式的动词：**
- want, wish, hope, expect, decide, determine, plan, agree, refuse, promise, manage, afford, pretend, offer, happen, seem, appear, tend

**两者皆可但意义不同的动词（高频考点）：**

| 动词 | + doing | + to do |
|------|---------|---------|
| remember | 记得做过（已做） | 记得要去做（未做） |
| forget | 忘记做过（已做） | 忘记要去做（未做） |
| stop | 停止正在做的事 | 停下来去做另一件事 |
| try | 尝试做某事 | 努力/设法做某事 |
| regret | 后悔做了某事 | 遗憾地要做某事 |
| mean | 意味着 | 打算做 |
| go on | 继续做同一件事 | 接着做另一件事 |

**示例：**
- I remember **locking** the door.（我记得锁了门——已锁）
- Remember **to lock** the door.（记得去锁门——还没锁）
- He stopped **smoking**.（他戒了烟）
- He stopped **to smoke**.（他停下来去抽烟）

#### 2.5.3 分词

**现在分词（doing）vs 过去分词（done）：**

| 对比项 | 现在分词 | 过去分词 |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 语态 | 主动 | 被动 |
| 时间 | 正在进行 | 已经完成 |
| 作定语 | the **sleeping** baby（正在睡的婴儿） | the **broken** window（被打碎的窗户） |
| 作表语 | The news is **exciting**. | I am **excited**. |
| 作宾补 | I saw him **crossing** the road.（正在进行） | I had my hair **cut**. |
| 作状语 | **Walking** along the street, I met a friend. | **Seen** from the hill, the city looks beautiful. |

> ⚠️ **高频考点**：-ing 表示"令人...的"，-ed 表示"感到...的"
> - interesting / interested, boring / bored, exciting / excited, surprising / surprised, disappointing / disappointed, confusing / confused, frightening / frightened, satisfying / satisfied, tiring / tired

**独立主格结构：**
- 结构：名词/代词 + 分词/形容词/副词/介词短语/不定式
- **Weather permitting**, we'll go on a picnic.（如果天气允许）
- **The meeting over**, everyone left.（会议结束后）
- **He came in**, book in **hand**.（手里拿着书）

## 3. 句法结构

### 3.1 句子成分与五大基本句型

| 句型 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| SV | 主语 + 谓语 | Birds **fly**. |
| SVO | 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 | I **love** music. |
| SVP | 主语 + 系动词 + 表语 | She **is** beautiful. |
| SVOO | 主语 + 谓语 + 间宾 + 直宾 | He **gave** me a book. |
| SVOC | 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 + 宾补 | We **elected** him chairman. |

### 3.2 主谓一致

#### 3.2.1 语法一致原则

| 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 不定式、动名词、从句作主语，谓语用单数 | **To see** is **to believe**. / **What he said** is true. |
| each, every, either, neither + 单数名词，谓语用单数 | **Each** student **has** a book. |
| many a / more than one + 单数名词，谓语用单数 | **Many a** student **has** passed the exam. |
| a number of + 复数名词 → 复数 | **A number of** students **are** absent. |
| the number of + 复数名词 → 单数 | **The number of** students **is** 50. |

#### 3.2.2 意义一致原则

| 规则 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 集合名词（family, class, team, public, government, audience）作整体→单数；强调成员→复数 | His family **is** large. / His family **are** all well. |
| the + 形容词表一类人→复数 | The old **are** respected. |
| 时间、金钱、距离作整体→单数 | Ten dollars **is** enough. / Five years **is** a long time. |
| 学科名/国名/书名/组织名→单数 | Physics **is** difficult. / The United States **is** a big country. |

#### 3.2.3 就近原则

| 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| either...or | Either you or he **is** wrong. |
| neither...nor | Neither you nor he **is** wrong. |
| not only...but also | Not only the students but also the teacher **was** present. |
| there be | There **is** a book and two pens on the desk. |

#### 3.2.4 就远原则

以下结构不影响主语的数：as well as, together with, along with, rather than, except, besides, including, with

- The teacher, **together with** his students, **is** going to the park.
- Nobody **but** Tom **knows** the secret.

### 3.3 并列句

**常见并列连词：**

| 连词 | 关系 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| and | 并列/递进 | He is smart **and** hardworking. |
| but / yet | 转折 | He is poor **but** happy. |
| or / otherwise | 选择/否则 | Hurry up, **or** you'll be late. |
| so / therefore | 因果 | It rained, **so** we stayed home. |
| for | 原因（补充说明） | He must be ill, **for** he looks pale. |
| not only...but also | 递进 | **Not only** did he come, **but** he also helped. |
| either...or | 选择 | **Either** you go **or** I go. |
| neither...nor | 否定并列 | **Neither** you **nor** I am wrong. |
| both...and | 并列 | **Both** he **and** his wife are teachers. |

> ⚠️ **考点**：not only...but also 连接两个分句时，not only 部分要倒装。

### 3.4 名词性从句

名词性从句包括**主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句**。

#### 3.4.1 引导词

| 引导词 | 用法 |
|--------|------|
| that | 无意义，不作成分（宾语从句中可省略） |
| whether / if | 是否（if 只引导宾语从句；whether 可引导所有名词性从句） |
| what | …的东西/事（= the thing that） |
| who / whom / whose | 谁 |
| which | 哪一个 |
| when / where / why / how | 时间/地点/原因/方式 |
| whoever / whatever / whichever | 无论谁/什么/哪个 |

#### 3.4.2 主语从句

- **That** he passed the exam surprised us all.
- **It** is important **that** we (should) learn English well.（it 作形式主语）
- **What** he said made me angry.
- **Whether** he will come is unknown.

> ⚠️ **考点**：主语从句中 **that 不可省略**；**if 不能引导主语从句**（用 whether）。

#### 3.4.3 宾语从句

- I think **(that)** he is right.（that 可省略）
- I don't know **whether/if** he will come.
- Please tell me **what** you want.
- I wonder **why** he was late.

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - 宾语从句用**陈述语序**（不能倒装）。
> - 否定转移：I don't think he is right.（不说 I think he isn't right.）
> - **形式宾语 it**：I find **it** important **that** we should study hard.

#### 3.4.4 表语从句

- The problem is **that** we don't have enough money.
- This is **where** the accident happened.
- It looks **as if** it is going to rain.

#### 3.4.5 同位语从句

- The news **that** he had won the game excited us.
- I have no idea **when** he will return.

> ⚠️ **考点**：同位语从句 vs 定语从句
> - 同位语从句：that 无意义，不作成分，从句解释名词内容。The news **that** he won...（什么消息——他赢了）
> - 定语从句：that 作成分（主语/宾语），从句修饰限定名词。The news **that** he told me...（他告诉我的那个消息，that = which，作 told 的宾语）

---

### 3.5 定语从句

定语从句是 PETS-3 **必考语法**。

#### 3.5.1 关系代词

| 关系代词 | 先行词 | 在从句中作 |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| who | 人 | 主语/宾语 |
| whom | 人 | 宾语 |
| whose | 人/物 | 定语 |
| which | 物 | 主语/宾语 |
| that | 人/物 | 主语/宾语 |
| as | 人/物 | 主/宾/表（常与 such, the same, as 连用） |

#### 3.5.2 关系副词

| 关系副词 | 先行词 | 相当于 |
|----------|--------|--------|
| when | 时间名词 | in/on/at which |
| where | 地点名词 | in/at which |
| why | reason | for which |

**示例：**
- This is the house **where** I was born. = This is the house **in which** I was born.
- I'll never forget the day **when** I first came to Beijing.
- I don't know the reason **why** he was absent.

#### 3.5.3 只用 that 的情况（高频考点）

1. 先行词被**最高级**修饰：This is the best film **that** I have ever seen.
2. 先行词被**序数词**修饰：The first thing **that** we should do is...
3. 先行词被 **all, every, any, no, the only, the very** 修饰：All **that** glitters is not gold.
4. 先行词**既有人又有物**：The man and his dog **that** were crossing the street...
5. 主句是 **who/which** 开头的疑问句：Who is the girl **that** is standing there?

#### 3.5.4 只用 which 的情况

1. **非限制性定语从句**中：He failed the exam, **which** made his parents angry.
2. **介词 + which**：This is the house **in which** I was born.

#### 3.5.5 限制性 vs 非限制性定语从句

| 对比项 | 限制性 | 非限制性 |
|--------|--------|----------|
| 逗号 | 无 | 有逗号隔开 |
| 功能 | 不可去掉，影响意思 | 可去掉，补充说明 |
| that | 可用 | **不能用 that** |
| 翻译 | ...的 | 补充说明 |

**非限制性定语从句的特殊用法：**
- **which** 可指代整个主句：He arrived late, **which** annoyed the teacher.
- **as** 引导的非限制性定语从句可放句首：**As** is known to all, the earth is round.

> ⚠️ **考点**：as vs which 引导非限制性定语从句
> - **as** 可放句首、句中、句末，常译为"正如"：As is known, ... / He is late, as usual.
> - **which** 只能放主句之后：He was late, which made the teacher angry.

---

### 3.6 状语从句

#### 3.6.1 时间状语从句

| 连词 | 用法 | 注意事项 |
|------|------|----------|
| when | 当...时 | 可表突然性 |
| while | 在...期间 | 后接进行时或表对比 |
| as | 当...时/随着 | 强调同时性 |
| before | 在...之前 | It was + 时间 + before...（过了多久才...） |
| after | 在...之后 | |
| since | 自从 | 主句用完成时，从句用过去时 |
| until / till | 直到 | not...until...（直到...才...） |
| as soon as | 一...就... | |
| hardly...when / no sooner...than | 一...就... | hardly/no sooner 部分用过去完成时，倒装 |
| the moment / the instant / the minute | 一...就... | |
| every time / each time | 每次 | |
| by the time | 到...时为止 | 常用完成时 |

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - **Hardly had I arrived when** the phone rang.
> - **No sooner had he left than** it began to rain.
> - **It was three years before** he came back.（过了三年他才回来）
> - **It is three years since** he left.（他离开已经三年了）

#### 3.6.2 地点状语从句

- **where**：Where there is a will, there is a way.
- **wherever**：Wherever you go, I will follow you.

#### 3.6.3 原因状语从句

| 连词 | 语气 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| because | 最强，直接原因 | I stayed home **because** it rained. |
| since | 已知原因 | **Since** you're tired, let's stop. |
| as | 明显原因 | **As** it was late, we went home. |
| now that | 既然 | **Now that** you're here, let's begin. |
| seeing that / considering that | 鉴于 | **Seeing that** he's ill, he won't come. |

> ⚠️ **考点**：because 不能与 so 连用；although 不能与 but 连用。

#### 3.6.4 条件状语从句

| 连词 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| if | 如果 |
| unless (= if...not) | 除非 |
| as long as / so long as | 只要 |
| on condition that | 条件是 |
| provided/providing (that) | 假如 |
| suppose/supposing (that) | 假设 |
| in case | 万一 |

> ⚠️ **考点**：条件/时间状语从句中，用一般现在时代替将来时。

#### 3.6.5 目的状语从句

- **so that / in order that** + 情态动词（can/could/may/might/will/would）：He studied hard **so that** he could pass the exam.
- **for fear that** (唯恐)：He ran away **for fear that** he should be caught.
- **lest** (以免)：He ran away **lest** he (should) be caught.

#### 3.6.6 结果状语从句

- **so + adj./adv. + that**：He was **so tired that** he fell asleep.
- **such + (a/an) + n. + that**：It was **such a good film that** we all enjoyed it.
- **so that**（结果）：He didn't study, **so that** he failed the exam.

> ⚠️ **考点**：so...that vs such...that
> - **so** + adj./adv. + that
> - **such** + n.（含修饰语）+ that
> - 但：so + adj. + a/an + n.（= such a/an + adj. + n.）：so beautiful a girl = such a beautiful girl

#### 3.6.7 让步状语从句

| 连词 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| although / though | **Although** he is young, he is capable. |
| even though / even if | **Even if** it rains, I'll go. |
| while | **While** I understand your concern, I disagree. |
| as（倒装让步） | Young **as** he is, he knows a lot. |
| however + adj./adv. | **However** hard he tried, he failed. |
| whatever / whoever / whichever / whenever / wherever / no matter + wh- | **Whatever** you say, I won't believe you. |

> ⚠️ **考点**：as 引导让步状语从句时需倒装：
> - **Child as** he is, he knows a lot.（名词提前不加冠词）
> - **Try as** he might, he couldn't open the door.（动词原形提前）
> - **Much as** I like it, I won't buy it.（副词/形容词提前）

#### 3.6.8 方式状语从句

- **as**：Do **as** I say.
- **as if / as though**：He talks **as if** he knew everything.（虚拟语气）

#### 3.6.9 比较状语从句

- **than**：He is taller **than** I (am).
- **as...as**：He is **as** tall **as** his father.
- **not so/as...as**：He is **not as** tall **as** his father.
- **the more...the more**：**The more** you read, **the more** you learn.

## 4. 特殊句式

### 4.1 倒装句

#### 4.1.1 完全倒装（谓语全部提前）

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 地点/方位副词开头 | **Here** comes the bus. / **Away** flew the bird. / **Out** rushed the children. |
| there / here 开头 | **There** goes the bell. |
| 表语前置 | **Present at the meeting** was the headmaster. |

> ⚠️ **注意**：主语为代词时不倒装：Here **it** comes. / Away **he** went.

#### 4.1.2 部分倒装（助动词/情态动词提前）

| 情况 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| 否定词/半否定词开头 | **Never** have I seen such a thing. / **Seldom** does he go out. / **Hardly** had he arrived when... / **Not only** did he come, but he also helped. / **Not until** midnight did he finish. / **In no way** can I agree. / **By no means** should we give up. / **Under no circumstances** should you do that. |
| only + 状语开头 | **Only then** did I realize the truth. / **Only in this way** can you succeed. / **Only when** he told me did I know. |
| so / such 开头表"也" | He is a student. **So** am I. / He can swim. **So** can I. |
| neither / nor 开头表"也不" | He can't swim. **Neither/Nor** can I. |
| so...that 中 so 提前 | **So** beautiful **was** the scenery **that** we stopped to admire it. |
| as 引导让步从句 | **Young as** he is, he is capable. |
| 省略 if 的虚拟条件句 | **Were** I you... / **Had** I known... / **Should** it rain... |

> ⚠️ **考点**：
> - **So do I.**（我也一样）vs **So I do.**（确实如此——表示强调，不倒装）
>   - He works hard. — **So do I.**（我也努力）
>   - He works hard. — **So he does.**（他确实努力）
> - **Neither/Nor do I.**（我也不——倒装）

---

### 4.2 强调句

#### 4.2.1 It is/was...that/who... 强调句型

**结构**：It is/was + 被强调部分 + that/who + 其余部分

- 强调人可用 who/that：**It was Tom who/that** broke the window.
- 强调其他成分只能用 that：**It was yesterday that** I met him. / **It was in the park that** we met.

**判断方法**：去掉 It is/was 和 that/who 后，剩余部分能组成完整句子即为强调句。

> ⚠️ **考点**：强调句的疑问形式
> - **Was it** Tom **who/that** broke the window?
> - **Who was it that** broke the window?
> - **When was it that** you met him?

#### 4.2.2 其他强调方式

| 方式 | 示例 |
|------|------|
| do/does/did + 动词原形 | I **do** like English. / He **did** come yesterday. |
| what 引导（所...的） | **What** I need is a good rest. |
| 反身代词强调 | I **myself** did it. / I did it **myself**. |
| It is/was not until...that... | **It was not until** midnight **that** he finished. |

---

### 4.3 省略句

| 省略情况 | 示例 |
|----------|------|
| 状语从句中主语与主句相同且含 be | **While** (he was) **walking** in the park, he met a friend. / **If** (it is) **possible**, I'll help you. |
| 不定式的省略（保留 to） | —Would you like to come? —I'd love **to**. / You can go if you want **to**. |
| 并列句中重复部分 | He likes English and (he likes) math. |
| so / not 替代从句 | —Will it rain? —I think **so**. / I hope **not**. (= I hope it won't rain.) |
| 虚拟条件句省略 if | **Were** I you... / **Had** he known... |

---

### 4.4 there be 存在句

**结构**：There + be + 主语 + 地点/时间

- There **is** a book on the desk.
- There **are** many students in the classroom.
- There **seems/appears to be** a problem.
- There **used to be** a temple here.
- There **is no doubt** that...
- There **is no point/use** (in) doing...
- There **is no denying** that...

> ⚠️ **考点**：there be 句型中，be 的单复数由最近的主语决定（就近原则）：
> - There **is** a pen and two books on the desk.

## 5. 构词法

### 5.1 前缀

| 前缀 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| un- | 不、相反 | unhappy, unable, unusual |
| dis- | 不、否定 | disagree, disappear, dishonest |

## 6. 附录：易混淆考点速查表

### 6.1 易混时态对比

| 对比 | 说明 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时 | 完成时强调对现在的影响；过去时强调过去的时间 | I **have lost** my key.（现在没钥匙）/ I **lost** my key yesterday. |
| 现在完成时 vs 现在完成进行时 | 完成时强调结果；完成进行时强调持续过程 | I **have read** the book.（读完了）/ I **have been reading** the book.（还在读） |
| 一般过去时 vs 过去完成时 | 过去完成时是"过去的过去" | When I arrived, he **had left**.（先离开后到达） |
| will do vs be going to | will 临时决定/预测；be going to 计划/有迹象 | Look at the clouds! It **is going to** rain. |

### 6.2 易混从句对比

| 对比 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 同位语从句 vs 定语从句 | 同位语从句的 that 不作成分，解释名词内容；定语从句的 that 作成分，修饰限定名词 |
| so...that vs so that | so...that（如此...以至于）表结果；so that（以便/所以）表目的或结果 |
| what vs that（名词性从句） | what = the thing that（作成分）；that 不作成分 |
| as vs which（非限制性定语从句） | as 可放句首，译"正如"；which 只放主句后 |

### 6.3 易混词/短语对比

| 对比 | 区别 |
|------|------|
| used to do | 过去常常（现在不这样了） |
| be used to doing | 习惯于做某事 |
| be used to do | 被用来做某事 |
| spend...on/(in) doing | 人作主语 |
| cost... | 物作主语 |
| take...to do | it 作形式主语 |
| pay...for | 人作主语 |
| too...to... | 太...而不能... |
| so...that... | 如此...以至于... |
| enough to do | 足够...能做... |
| because / because of | because + 从句；because of + 名词/动名词 |
| although / but | 不能同时使用 |
| because / so | 不能同时使用 |
